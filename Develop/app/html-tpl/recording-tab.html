Studio A – Mixing Studio

Voyceo Studio A is a state-of-the-art music and post production studio with a large control room and a live tracking room. Equipped with a D-Control ICON, Pro Tools HDX, Tannoy System 215 DMT II mains, a multitude of new and vintage outboard gear, as well as a 110" retractable projection screen, 7.1 surround sound, and full HDMI routing capabilities, Studio A is suitable for any session.

Designed and tuned by the famed George Augspurger, Studio A is great for any type of audio work.
